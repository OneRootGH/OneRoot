Operating Notes

How To Work With This App

Use Expenses for normal purchases and services paid by any method.

Use Daily Sales for one total amount per business area each day.

Use Apartments for rent and monthly bills. For a bills-only month, leave the rent fields at 0.00 and fill only the bill fields.

Use Generate Monthly Bill Records to create the next month from the latest apartment profile without replacing existing records.

Use Petty Cash for float movements and small cash spending; expense-paid entries sync into Expenses.

Use Salary to manage monthly payroll, deductions, part-payments, and outstanding staff balances by business area.

Use Suppliers to track what you owe, what you have paid, and what is overdue with vendors.

Use Recurring to generate repeated expense, supplier, maintenance, or apartment billing records when they fall due.

Use Maintenance to track repairs, cleaning tasks, technicians, open work, and completion costs.
